Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Can Crush ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the Can Crush mobile application.

Information We Collect

When using Can Crush, users may upload images of crushed cans and coins used for scale. These images are processed to measure the diameter of crushed cans.

The app may also collect:

  • Username or profile information
  • Uploaded images
  • Leaderboard statistics
  • Basic usage analytics

How We Use Information

Information collected is used to:

  • Measure crushed cans
  • Display leaderboard rankings
  • Improve app functionality
  • Prevent fraud or abuse

Data Storage

Images and associated data may be stored securely using cloud infrastructure providers.

Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services for analytics, hosting, and infrastructure.
