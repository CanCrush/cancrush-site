How it works
  1. Crush a can as flat as you can.
  2. Place a standard card (ID, credit card) next to it for scale.
  3. Open Can Crush and snap the photo.
  4. The app measures your crush instantly on your phone.
  5. Share your score and talk your trash.

How competitions work
Create a competition and invite friends, or start a Tailgate Mode competition that anyone nearby can join. Set a time window, pick a radius, and watch the entries roll in. Each competition has its own leaderboard with tabs for every can type that gets crushed. Or send a Beat My Crush challenge — share your best crush and dare friends to beat it, with no time limit.
Privacy first
Your card is used only as a size reference — Can Crush masks the card pixels before anything leaves your phone. No card data is ever transmitted or stored. Your crush photo (with the card blacked out) is the only image that reaches our servers.
